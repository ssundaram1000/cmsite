How We Work ?

Our methodology is very robust and has helped us deliver high profile projects for Fortune 500 compainies.

  • Idea

    We start with brainstorming a solution together or get into the details on your idea for existing or new application. We can also come in to do an assessment of your application portfolio and infrastructure landscape.

  • Analysis

    We analyze the information received, carefully document the choices and collaborate with you in defining a solution or roadmap.

  • Plan

    Detailed task breakdown and planning will help us come up with a defined structure to execute the project. We account for unknowns and risks and build triggers and process for communication and course correction.

  • Build

    We design and build the application in an iterative fashion with periodic checkins to demo work in progress.

  • Test

    Test planning starts during the plan phase, where we clearly document the functional, system and user acceptance test. We will provide you reports of defects found and speed at which they are being addressed.

  • Launch

    Launch checklist is created well in advance. We will monitor the system for any post launch issues.
